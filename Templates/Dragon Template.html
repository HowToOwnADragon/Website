Spawning

The DRAGON NAME is a fairly common dragon. It usually spawns in groups of 4 in the following biomes:

  • Plains
  • Sunflower Plains
  • Snowy Plains
  • Windswept Hills
  • Windswept Forest
  • Windswept Gravelly Hills
  • Snowy Slopes
  • Stony Peaks
  • Stony Shore
  • Grove

Colors

The DRAGON NAME can spawn in a variety of different colors, including:

  • Blue
  • Orange
  • Purple
  • Brown (Doesn't spawn naturally, will be elaborated on later in the development)

The pictures of these versions can be found in the gallery at the bottom of this page

Drops

When killed, the DRAGON NAME drops scales depending on their current growth stage.

Stage Amount of scales dropped:
Baby 0/1 (50% chance to drop a scale)
Juvenile 0/1 (50% chance to drop a scale)
Adolescent 0/1 (50% chance to drop a scale)
Fully Grown 0-2 (33% chance for nothing, 33% for 1 scale and 33% for 2 scales)

Behaviour

DRAGON NAMEs are quite peaceful in general. They just wander around in groups, doing nothing.

Their behaviour will be expanded on in a later update.

Taming & Riding

To tame a DRAGON NAME, right-click the DRAGON NAME a couple of times with one of the following items:

  • Stone
  • Cobblestone
  • Deepslate
  • Cobbled Deepslate
  • Sandstone
  • Andesite
  • Diorite
  • Granite
  • Cod
  • Salmon

Then, if tamed, Right-click the DRAGON NAME with an empty hand to ride it.

Note that you do not need a saddle to ride and control the DRAGON NAME. It is completely optional and so far completely cosmetic.

Breeding & Growing

To breed a DRAGON NAME, use one of the following items on a male and female DRAGON NAME and they'll create an egg. Put this egg into an Incubator, wait 30 seconds and then put a lava bucket in the bottom slot (the egg does not have to be taken out of the incubator) and click the "Hatch" button.

After a DRAGON NAME has laid an egg, the Female will have a cooldown of 30 seconds. Luckily the male won't have a cooldown at all.

Breed items:

  • Stone
  • Cobblestone
  • Deepslate
  • Cobbled Deepslate
  • Andesite
  • Diorite
  • Granite
  • Sandstone

When a DRAGON NAME egg hatches, a Baby DRAGON NAME spawns. The baby has a 50/50 chance to be either Male or Female, and a 50/50 chance to have the color of either parent. It will automatically be tamed by the person that hatched the egg.

The DRAGON NAME has 4 Stages, Baby, Juvenile, Adolescent and Fully Grown. Every stage can take around 30 seconds to 2.5 minutes. At the end of every stage, the DRAGON NAME will ask for one of the items below. After you give the correct item to the DRAGON NAME, the next stage will spawn pre-tamed by the person that fed them the item.

Possible items to give the DRAGON NAME to grow up when prompted:

  • Stone
  • Cobblestone
  • Deepslate
  • Cobbled Deepslate
  • Andesite
  • Diorite
  • Granite
  • Sandstone
  • Bucket of Lava
  • Iron Ingot

Abilities

The DRAGON NAME has (as of alpha 0.0.6) only one ability, which is to fire a Fireball from it's mouth by pressing the ability keybind (R by default). This can be done up to 6 times in a row, after which the DRAGON NAME needs to "rest" for five minutes before it can fire again.
