Behaviour

Deadly Nadders are quite peaceful in general. They just wander around in groups, doing nothing.

Their behaviour will be expanded on in a later update.

Drops

When killed, the Deadly Nadder drops scales and spines depending on their current growth stage. This is influenced by the Looting enchantment. Deadly Nadders drop their scales and their spines.

Scale Drops
Stage Amount of scales dropped:
Baby 0/1 (50% chance to drop a scale)
Juvenile 0/1 (50% chance to drop a scale)
Adolescent 0/1 (50% chance to drop a scale)
Fully Grown 0-2 (33% chance for nothing, 33% for 1 scale and 33% for 2 scales)

Spine Drops
Stage Amount of spines dropped:
Baby None
Juvenile None
Adolescent None
Fully Grown 0-2 (33% chance for nothing, 33% for 1 spine and 33% for 2 spine)

Deadly Nadder Scales are used to craft Valka's Staff and a Deadly Nadder Saddle.

Their Spines are used to craft Deadly Nadder Arrows.

Taming & Riding

To tame a Deadly Nadder, right-click on it a couple of times with one of the following items:

Taming Food(s)
Raw Mutton
Raw Cod
Raw Salmon
Cooked Chicken

Then, if tamed, Right-click the Deadly Nadder with an empty hand to ride it.

Note that you do not need a saddle to ride and control the Deadly Nadder. It is completely optional and so far completely cosmetic.

Breeding & Growing

To breed a Deadly Nadder, use one of the following items on a male and female Deadly Nadder and they'll create an egg. Put this egg into an Incubator, wait 30 seconds and then put Flint in the bottom slot (the egg does not have to be taken out of the incubator) and click the "Hatch" button.

After a Deadly Nadder laid an egg the Female will have a cooldown of 30 seconds. Luckily the male won't have a cooldown at all.

Breeding item(s)
Raw Mutton
Raw Cod
Raw Salmon
Cooked Chicken

When a Deadly Nadder egg hatches, a Baby Deadly Nadder spawns. The baby has a 50/50 chance to be either Male or Female, and a 50/50 chance to have the color of either parent. It will automatically be tamed by the person that hatched the egg.

The Deadly Nadder has 4 Stages, Baby, Juvenile, Adolescent and Fully Grown. Every stage can take around 30 seconds to 2.5 minutes. At the end of every stage, the Deadly Nadder will ask for one of the items below. After you give the correct item to the Nadder, the next stage will spawn pre-tamed by the person that fed them the item.

Possible items to give the Deadly Nadder to grow up when prompted:

Prompting item(s)
Raw Mutton
Raw Cod
Raw Salmon
Cooked Chicken

Abilities

The Deadly Nadder (as of alpha 0.0.6) has no abilities. The Deadly Nadder's attacks will be added later on in development.
