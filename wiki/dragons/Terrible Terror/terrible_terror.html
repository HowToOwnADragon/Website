    Behaviour

    Terrible Terrors are quite peaceful in general. They just wander around in groups, doing nothing.

    Their behaviour will be expanded on in a later update.

    Drops

    When killed, the Terrible Terror drops scales depending on their current growth stage. This is influenced by the Looting enchantment.

    Scale Drops
    Stage Amount of scales dropped:
    Baby 0/1 (50% chance to drop a scale)
    Juvenile 0/1 (50% chance to drop a scale)
    Adolescent 0/1 (50% chance to drop a scale)
    Fully Grown 0-2 (33% chance for nothing, 33% for 1 scale and 33% for 2 scales)

    Taming & Riding

    To tame a Terrible Terror, right-click on it a couple of times with one of the following items:

    Taming Food(s)
    Bread
    Raw Cod
    Raw Salmon
    Raw Mutton

    Then, if tamed, Right-click the Terrible Terror with an empty hand to ride it.

    Note that you do not need a saddle to ride and control the Terrible Terror. It is completely optional and so far completely cosmetic.

    Breeding & Growing

    To breed a Terrible Terror, use one of the following items on a male and female Terrible Terror and they'll create an egg. Put this egg into an Incubator, wait 30 seconds and then put a Rabbit's Foot in the bottom slot (the egg does not have to be taken out of the incubator) and click the "Hatch" button.

    After a Terrible Terror laid an egg the Female will have a cooldown of 30 seconds. Luckily the male won't have a cooldown at all.

    Breeding item(s)
    Bread
    Raw Cod
    Raw Salmon
    Raw Mutton

    When a Terrible Terror egg hatches, a Baby Terrible Terror spawns. The baby has a 50/50 chance to be either Male or Female, and a 50/50 chance to have the color of either parent. It will automatically be tamed by the person that hatched the egg.

    The Terrible Terror has 4 Stages, Baby, Juvenile, Adolescent and Fully Grown. Every stage can take around 30 seconds to 2.5 minutes. At the end of every stage, the Terrible Terror will ask for one of the items below. After you give the correct item to the Terror, the next stage will spawn pre-tamed by the person that fed them the item.

    Possible items to give the Terrible Terror to grow up when prompted:

    Prompting item(s)
    Bread
    Raw Cod
    Raw Salmon
    Raw Mutton

    Abilities

    The Terrible Terror (as of alpha 0.0.6) has no abilities. The Terrible Terror attacks will be added later on in development
