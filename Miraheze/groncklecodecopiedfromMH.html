    Spawning

    The Gronckle is a fairly common dragon. It usually spawns in groups of 4 in the following biomes:

    • Plains
    • Sunflower Plains
    • Snowy Plains
    • Windswept Hills
    • Windswept Forest
    • Windswept Gravelly Hills
    • Snowy Slopes
    • Stony Peaks
    • Stony Shore
    • Grove

    Colors

    The Gronckle can spawn in a variety of different colors, including:

    • Blue
    • Orange
    • Purple
    • Brown (Doesn't spawn naturally, will be elaborated on later in the development)

    The pictures of these versions can be found in the gallery at the bottom of this page

    Drops

    When killed, the Gronckle drops scales depending on their current growth stage.

    Stage Amount of scales dropped:
    Baby 0/1 (50% chance to drop a scale)
    Juvenile 0/1 (50% chance to drop a scale)
    Adolescent 0/1 (50% chance to drop a scale)
    Fully Grown 0-2 (33% chance for nothing, 33% for 1 scale and 33% for 2 scales)

    Behaviour

    Gronckles are quite peaceful in general. They just wander around in groups, doing nothing.

    Their behaviour will be expanded on in a later update.

    Taming & Riding

    To tame a Gronckle, right-click the Gronckle a couple of times with one of the following items:

    • Stone
    • Cobblestone
    • Deepslate
    • Cobbled Deepslate
    • Sandstone
    • Andesite
    • Diorite
    • Granite
    • Cod
    • Salmon

    Then, if tamed, Right-click the Gronckle with an empty hand to ride it.

    Note that you do not need a saddle to ride and control the Gronckle. It is completely optional and so far completely cosmetic.

    Breeding & Growing

    To breed a Gronckle, use one of the following items on a male and female Gronckle and they'll create an egg. Put this egg into an Incubator, wait 30 seconds and then put a lava bucket in the bottom slot (the egg does not have to be taken out of the incubator) and click the "Hatch" button.

    After a Gronckle laid an egg the Female will have a cooldown of 30 seconds. Luckily the male won't have a cooldown at all.

    Breed items:

    • Stone
    • Cobblestone
    • Deepslate
    • Cobbled Deepslate
    • Andesite
    • Diorite
    • Granite
    • Sandstone

    When a Gronckle egg hatches, a Baby Gronckle spawns. The baby has a 50/50 chance to be either Male or Female, and a 50/50 chance to have the color of either parent. It will automatically be tamed by the person that hatched the egg.

    The Gronckle has 4 Stages, Baby, Juvenile, Adolescent and Fully Grown. Every stage can take around 30 seconds to 2.5 minutes. At the end of every stage, the Gronckle will ask for one of the items below. After you give the correct item to the Gronckle, the next stage will spawn pre-tamed by the person that fed them the item.

    Possible items to give the Gronckle to grow up when prompted:

    • Stone
    • Cobblestone
    • Deepslate
    • Cobbled Deepslate
    • Andesite
    • Diorite
    • Granite
    • Sandstone
    • Bucket of Lava
    • Iron Ingot

    Abilities

    The Gronckle has (as of alpha 0.0.6) only one ability, which is to fire a Fireball from it's mouth by pressing the ability keybind (R by default). This can be done up to 6 times in a row, after which the Gronckle needs to "rest" for five minutes before it can fire again.
